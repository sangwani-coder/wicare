Subscriber Role

As a Wicare subscriber, you will receive regular updates on the progress and impact of our programs. You will also have access to exclusive content, including success stories and volunteer opportunities.

Donor Role

As a Wicare donor, you play a critical role in helping us achieve our mission of eradicating poverty in Zambia. Your contributions go directly towards supporting families in need of financial assistance for basic needs such as food, clothing, and education. Through your support, you can help us break the cycle of poverty.

Wicare Role

At Wicare, we are committed to connecting donors with families in need in a transparent and efficient manner. We work with local communities to identify families who require assistance, and we ensure that all donations are used for their intended purpose. We also provide education, training, and access to financial resources to help families become self-sufficient.
