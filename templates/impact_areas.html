Our Impact Areas

We believe that everyone deserves access to basic needs, such as education, health, and clean water. WiCare is committed to achieving the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) through our work in the following five impact areas:

No Poverty

"Together, let's make poverty a thing of the past."

Our aim is to eradicate poverty in poor communities by connecting donors with families in need of financial assistance for basic needs such as food, clothing, and education....Our goal is to reduce poverty in Zambia by providing families with the necessary resources to support themselves. We work with communities to provide education, training, and access to financial resources. Through our platform, donors can support families in need and help to break the cycle of poverty.

Zero Hunger

"Join the movement for a world with zero hunger."

We work to provide food security and improve access to nutritious food in poor communities, reducing malnutrition and hunger-related illnesses.... We believe that everyone should have access to nutritious food. Our platform connects donors with families in need, allowing them to provide food and other essentials. We also work with communities to promote sustainable agriculture and provide education on nutrition and healthy eating.

Good Health and Well-being

"Empowering health and well-being for all."

We aim to improve the health and well-being of individuals in poor communities by providing access to basic healthcare services, including medical supplies and equipment.... We aim to improve the health and well-being of families in Zambia by providing access to basic healthcare and hygiene resources. Through our platform, donors can provide families with access to clean water, sanitation facilities, and medical supplies. We also work with communities to provide education on health and hygiene practices.

Quality Education

"Unlocking the potential of quality education for every child."

We strive to provide access to quality education for children in poor communities, ensuring they receive a well-rounded education that enables them to break the cycle of poverty and improve their future prospects.... We believe that education is key to breaking the cycle of poverty. Our platform connects donors with schools and families in need of education resources. We also work with communities to promote access to education and provide training and support for teachers.

Clean Water and Sanitation

"Providing clean water and sanitation, one drop at a time."

We work to improve access to clean water and sanitation facilities in poor communities, reducing the incidence of water-borne diseases and improving overall health and well-being.... Access to clean water and sanitation facilities is essential for good health and well-being. Through our platform, donors can provide families and schools with access to clean water and sanitation facilities. We also work with communities to promote sustainable water and sanitation practices.
